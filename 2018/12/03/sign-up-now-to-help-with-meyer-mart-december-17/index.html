Sign Up now to help with Meyer Mart, December 17!

If you would like to help out at the Meyer Mart event on Monday, December 17, sign up here:
Meyer Mart Sign Up

The students will be collecting spare change and bringing it to school Monday December 3 through Friday December 14. Our volunteers will use this money to buy wish list items for local families in need this holiday season. During Meyer Mart, the students will “shop” for the items at the store we set up at the school, and will pay with pretend money. Every classroom will get a turn at the Mart, practicing kind behavior.
